thoughts on poverty

Our mortality is a unique experience in comparison to our premortal existence. In here, many shortcomings, misgivings and trials are attached. Poverty is one of most prevalent temporal trials that plague our world. It can also manifest in numerous ways in the various facets of our lives.

Fighting off poverty is a tough and lengthy coure as it threatens the most fundamental aspects where our survival on this earthly journey lie.

its limitations ðŸ¦Ÿ

When you simply have no means to a safe community, you endanger the lives of your family and yourself. You begin to live in fear, rendering you disabled to favorable circumstances life can provide. When you lack the resources to pursue knowledge and education, you limit yourself to the many doors that will save you from this poor temporal state. When you have no access to food, nourishment, medication and professional help that will sustain your overall wellbeing, you risk your love ones and yourself the grief and pain that come with it. When you are financially poor, you lower yourself to a social standing and settle on the tightened corners of society's factional divide.

Poverty is not just an individual- or family-scale impoverishment. It is a plague that progressively erodes our self-worth, the one that sits on the very heart of our behavior, deeds, desires and ambitions.

its dangers ðŸ’€

As we're plagued by this condition, we imperil ourselves to understanding and valuing life incorrectly. Because of the scarcity of temporal resources, your pursuit becomes the acquisition of these things. You might begin to place a greater meaning to money, obsess about mundane details such as a good and steady career, fantasize and romanticize a luxurious lifestyle, and make it your ultimate objective to gain a high social status, granting you access to the network of like-minded people's resources. And while these things are not necessarily evil, they mislead you into thinking that you are safe, secure and cocooned in the arms of the natural world which will soon crumble.

Poverty can also be excellent teacher of hope. It is only through our nothingness that you recognize the everythingness of your Father in heaven. Only by our lowliness does He open the windows and doors of heaven, showering you in abundance of blessings, gifts, and love you may never expect and comprehend.
